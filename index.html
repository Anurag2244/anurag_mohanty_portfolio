About

Where product thinking meets technical craft

I approach every problem like a PM — starting with the user, reasoning backwards to a solution, and shipping something that measurably works.

I'm a product manager with a computer science foundation — someone equally comfortable writing a PRD, sketching a user flow in Figma, running a Python script to validate data, or architecting an AI agent with LangChain. Product and engineering aren't separate worlds to me; they're two sides of the same problem.

My time at IQnext — a B2B SaaS startup building smart building and IoT infrastructure — gave me hands-on ownership of real challenges: designing access control systems across multiple locations, leading an AI copilot experience from concept to release, and automating configuration workflows that previously demanded significant manual effort. I worked inside Agile sprints alongside engineering daily, not just as a liaison, but as someone who could go deep on both the "what" and the "how."

What drives me is the intersection of AI and product experience — building tools that don't just automate tasks but genuinely reduce cognitive load. I'm looking for opportunities where that combined perspective can create outsized impact.

Internal Platform

Internal Product Platform

Designed and shipped an end-to-end internal product operations platform at IQnext — a suite of four interconnected tools that automated knowledge management, competitive monitoring, roadmap visibility, and feature governance. The goal: reduce the operational overhead on the product team while giving every department reliable access to product intelligence.

Problem

Product knowledge was scattered, competitive awareness was reactive, roadmap updates required manual reformatting, and feature history had no single source of truth.

Approach

Identified high-friction touchpoints through team interviews, prioritised by impact-to-effort ratio, and shipped four targeted tools as a unified internal platform — no extra overhead, no new tab proliferation.

Platform Deliverables

01 / AI Feature Explainer
Automated query resolution for product knowledge
Deployed an AI-powered tool that ingests product documentation and resolves employee queries autonomously — eliminating repetitive questions routed to the product team.
~20% reduction in PM overhead
02 / Competitor Intelligence Module
Real-time competitive monitoring, embedded in the portal
Built a module that aggregates and surfaces competitor updates on the internal portal — shifting the team's competitive awareness from reactive to structured and ongoing.
Proactive positioning in sprint planning
03 / Automated Roadmap Visualisation
Upload-to-visual pipeline for roadmap distribution
A pipeline that detects newly uploaded roadmaps and auto-generates visual representations on the portal within seconds — replacing manual reformatting and version-control headaches.
Zero manual effort for roadmap distribution
04 / Master Feature Tracker & Request Hub
Single source of truth for shipped features and new requests
A dual-purpose system documenting 100% of shipped features with structured metadata, plus a cross-company module where employees can submit and upvote feature requests.
